CCGrid 2026 Posters

We invite participants to submit original and unpublished works as posters to the CCGrid 2026 conference, presenting the latest breakthroughs in Cluster, Cloud and Internet computing. Topics should align with the conference scope and tracks.

Submission and Review Process

Submit short (2–4 pages) poster papers in the IEEE conference template via EasyChair (select the CCGrid2026 Posters track). Submissions are peer-reviewed and evaluated on:

  • Quality of presentation
  • Scientific content
  • Relevance to the conference

Accepted poster papers’ authors must register and present their posters at the conference. Accepted papers will appear in the conference proceedings.

Best Poster Award

A Best Poster Award will be presented to the most accomplished CCGrid 2026 poster selected by the Poster PC. The winner receives a certificate and a prize.

Important Dates

  • Poster papers submission: 15 February 2026 28 February 2026
  • Notification of Acceptance: 5 March 2026
  • Final (camera-ready) version due / registration: 15 March 2026
  • Conference dates / poster presentation: 18–21 May 2026

Poster Presentation Information

  • By submitting, you agree to present the poster at CCGrid 2026 in Sydney, Australia.
  • Presenters must be registered CCGrid 2026 participants (only registered presenters’ posters are admitted).
  • Posters will be displayed during the conference; presenters may give a brief presentation.
  • Size/format: Max A0 portrait (841×1189 mm) or A1 portrait (594×841 mm).
  • Presenters create, print, and transport posters. Organizers provide boards, numbers, topic areas, tape, etc.
