CCGRID 2026 Travel Grants

With the support of Western Sydney University, a limited number of student travel grants are available to assist students in attending IEEE CCGrid 2026. This award can be used to help reduce the cost of airfare, travel to/from the conference venue, accommodations, and conference registration. Priority will be given to those having an accepted paper or poster in the conference or workshops, and to enhance the diversity of participants.

Application deadline is April 24, 2026 (AOE).
Award notifications will be sent by April 30, 2026.

This award will provide up to 1,500 AUD per student to support travel to the CCGrid 2026 conference. This award can be used to help reduce the cost of airfare, travel to/from the conference venue, accommodations, and conference registration. The award will be processed as a reimbursement at the end of the conference, so please save all receipts associated with conference attendance.

Award Expectations

Students receiving the award are expected to actively participate in the conference. As per the rules, the awardee must attend all sessions of the conference throughout its entire duration of 4 days.

Award Restrictions

This travel award is only available to current students studying at institutions of higher education worldwide.

Airfare Restrictions

Airfare must be purchased in the lowest available fare class. Travellers who fly premium economy/business/first class must document the lowest available economy fare and may allocate the difference using personal funds.

Incomplete applications will not be considered for the award.
