CCGrid 2026 — Patron Program

The IEEE International Symposium on Cluster, Cloud, and Internet Computing (CCGrid) is one of the flagship international conferences in distributed systems. Becoming a patron of CCGrid 2026 is more than just sponsorship — it is an opportunity to improve your company’s visibility, connect with global leaders, and attract top talent.

We offer multiple ways to get involved, tailored to organisations of all sizes and priorities. Whether your goal is to build brand visibility, engage talent, or shape the future of innovation, we have a patron package that fits.

Patron Levels & Benefits

Platinum

$10,000 and above
  • Talk during the conference
  • Designated exhibit table (entire conference)
  • 2 complimentary conference registrations
  • 3 complimentary tutorial registrations
  • A display board at the conference venue
  • Company name and logo included in the IEEE Conference Proceedings (if on time) and flyer distributed to the audience
  • Presence on the conference website

Gold

$5,000 to $10,000
  • 1 complimentary conference registration
  • 2 complimentary tutorial registrations
  • A display board at the conference venue
  • Company name and logo included in the IEEE Conference Proceedings (if on time) and flyer distributed to the audience
  • Presence on the conference website

Silver

$3,000 to $5,000
  • 2 complimentary tutorial registrations
  • A display board at the conference venue
  • Company name and logo included in the IEEE Conference Proceedings (if on time) and flyer distributed to the audience
  • Presence on the conference website
Alternatively, a patron may choose to sponsor specific events and activities (e.g., student registration and/or travel grants, coffee breaks, lunch, reception, or conference banquet) or offer an in-kind contribution.

For enquiries, please contact the Local Arrangements Chair at k.jagarlamudi@westernsydney.edu.au.

Why CCGrid?

Established over two decades ago, CCGrid brings together the brightest minds from academia, industry, and government. In 2026, the symposium will be hosted in Sydney, with Parramatta as the hub of activities. This milestone event builds on the legacy of recent editions in Tromsø, Norway (2025); Philadelphia, USA (2024); and Bangalore, India (2023). Previous editions proudly partnered with industry leaders, including Nokia and other multinational corporations, reinforcing CCGrid’s reputation as an influential meeting ground for cutting-edge innovation.

This is your chance to:

  • Showcase your brand to an international audience of researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders.
  • Demonstrate thought leadership and alignment with the latest innovations in cloud, edge, AI, and IoT.
  • Engage top-tier talent, including graduate students and early-career researchers — the future innovators of our field.
  • Support global R&D efforts that drive impactful, real-world applications.
