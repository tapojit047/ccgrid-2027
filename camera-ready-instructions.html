Camera-Ready Instructions

Congratulations on having your paper accepted at CCGRID 2026 and its co-located workshops and ICFEC 2026! This page has common instructions for submitting your camera-ready paper for publication in the proceedings for any of these events.

For your accepted paper, one of the co-authors of the paper (authorized by the other co-authors) should follow the steps below to submit the final version (the camera-ready). We use the IEEE Conference Publication Services (CPS) to collect the camera-ready paper.

Requirements for Publication in CCGrid 2026 Proceedings and IEEE Xplore®

You need to complete the following steps:

  1. At least one author registers for the conference at the full author rate
  2. Ensure formatting and verification of the IEEE Xplore® compliance (see below for PDF eXpress instructions)
  3. Upload your camera-ready following the instructions on the relevant IEEE CPS submission page, including the copyright release.

⚠️ Deadline: The deadline for completing all these steps is March 22, 2026.

For all camera-ready paper types, prepare your paper using the IEEE conference proceedings template (letter size: 8.5 x 11 inches), and use PDF eXpress for validation and/or conversion of your camera-ready files to PDF format. Please use the correct conference IDs for your relevant paper type. You can also find paper preparation and validation instructions at the camera-ready submission site.
