Venue and Stay

📍 Conference Venue

The IEEE/ACM International Symposium on Cluster, Cloud and Internet Computing (CCGrid 2026) will be held at Western Sydney University, Parramatta City Campus (Building 1 - Peter Shergold Building). Conference sessions will take place on Level 9.

Venue Address:
Western Sydney University, Parramatta City Campus (1PSQ)
169 Macquarie Street
Parramatta NSW 2150
Australia

🚶 The venue is located a 5-minute walk from Parramatta Train Station.

🚊 Getting to the Venue

From Sydney Airport (SYD):

  • Train (recommended): Airport Link train to Parramatta Station (approximately 45–60 minutes)
  • Taxi or car: Approximately 35–50 minutes depending on traffic

From Parramatta Station, the venue at 1 Parramatta Square is within walking distance. For more information, please visit the campus information page .

🏨 Accommodation

Participants are encouraged to stay in Parramatta CBD for convenient access to the conference venue. Western Sydney University has arranged discounted accommodation rates at selected hotels for CCGrid 2026 participants.

📅 Recommended Stay Period:

Check-in: 17 May 2026
Check-out: 22 May 2026
