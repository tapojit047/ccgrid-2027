Registration Policy

  • Conference/Workshop/ICFEC Author registration deadline is March 22, 2026. Non-author early registration deadline is April 15, 2026 April 30, 2026.
  • At least one author of an accepted conference/workshop/ICFEC paper must register as an Author at the "IEEE/ACM member" or "Non-Member" price by March 22, 2026.
  • An author with multiple accepted conference papers may have a discount of AUD 400 for each additional paper registration.
  • Member registration rates are available for authors with professional members of IEEE/ACM or ACM SIGHPC.
  • Conference paper page limit is 10. Up to 2 extra pages can be purchased with a fee (AUD 200 per page).
  • Tutorial Day registration is only for "Non-Author" who are interested in attending the first day of the conference (May 18, 2026), including workshop sessions and tutorials.
  • Local Student Registration (Non-Author) is a special rate available to current students in Australia. Please note that the conference dinner is not included, but tickets can be purchased separately.
  • Registration can be cancelled on or before April 20, 2026. There will be an administrative fee of AUD 150 deducted from each refund.
  • Registration includes access to conference and workshop rooms, proceedings, food (lunches and breaks), conference dinner, social event, bag and one-week transport ticket.
  • The official invitation letter can be requested during registration.
  • For registration questions, please email registrations@catalystevents.com.au
